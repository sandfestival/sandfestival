Sand Festival Montenegro

International Sand Sculpture Exhibition in Kotor / Tivat

About the Project

Sand Festival Montenegro is a seasonal open-air exhibition featuring monumental sand sculptures created by international artists. The project combines art, tourism, education and family activities.

Activities

  • Large-scale sand sculptures
  • Children’s sand playground
  • Workshops and masterclasses
  • Educational programs
  • Café & relaxation area
  • Live performances and evening events
